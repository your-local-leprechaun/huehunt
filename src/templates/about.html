What is HueHunt?

Every day, a new color gets picked. Your job is to go find something in the real world that matches it, as you see it. Snap a photo, submit it, and see how everyone else interpreted the same color. You won't see anyone else's until you post your own though!

One color, a hundred different ways of seeing it.

How it Works

  1. A new color is generated every day, the same one for everyone.
  2. Go find something in the real world that matches it and take a photo.
  3. Submit your photo to unlock the gallery and see everyone else's take.
  4. Come back tomorrow to keep your streak alive.

Gamifying Exploration and Appreciation

Most of us move through the world without really looking at it. HueHunt is built around the idea that a small, specific challenge is all it takes to change that. When you're hunting for a color, everything becomes worth a second glance. The warm terracotta of a brick wall, the grey-green of peeling bench paint, the way afternoon light completely changes a color you thought you knew. It's a small shift in attention, but it adds up.

The daily challenge gives you a reason to go looking every single day, turning an ordinary walk into something worth paying attention to. Streaks reward you for showing up consistently, because that's exactly how you build a sharper eye over time. The gallery is locked until you submit your own photo, so your take on the color stays uninfluenced. And then once it opens up, seeing how differently everyone interpreted the exact same hex code is genuinely surprising every time. It makes you want to go out and try again tomorrow.
