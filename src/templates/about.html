What is HueHunt?

Every day, a single hex color drops. Your job is to go find something in the real world that matches it — as you see it. Snap a picture, submit it, and see how everyone else interpreted the same color. Just remember you can't see anyone else's perspective till you post your own!

One color interpreted in unique ways.

A new appreciation for color

Most of us move through the world without really looking at it. We see a park bench, a coffee cup, a brick wall — but we don't notice the exact warm terracotta of the brick, or the particular muted grey-green of the bench paint peeling in the corner.

HueHunt gives you a reason to stop and look. When you're hunting for a specific color, everything becomes worth a second glance. You start noticing the subtle differences between shades, the way light changes a color entirely, the surprising places color hides. It's a small shift in attention that changes how you experience the world around you.

Why gamification works

🎯
A daily reason to explore

The challenge gives you something specific to look for every single day. It turns an ordinary walk into a hunt, and an ordinary environment into somewhere worth exploring.

🔥
Streaks build habits

The streak mechanic keeps you coming back — and consistency is exactly how you train a sharper eye. The longer you play, the more naturally you start noticing color everywhere.

👀
The gallery changes how you see the challenge

Seeing fifty different photos of the same hex code shows you just how much interpretation goes into color perception. It makes you want to look harder next time.
